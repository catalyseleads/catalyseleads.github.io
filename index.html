Businesses

Our matching fee starts at 10% of all revenue from customers sourced through our platform. That includes the ~3% fee we pay to accept credit cards. We handle all the nitty gritty details of one-time (or recurring) payments, so you can focus on building the best product possible. We'll help the right customers find your product and that you get your hard earned money. We'll send you a biweekly ACH transfer for customer payments less our fee. Win-win.
